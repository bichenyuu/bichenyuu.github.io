## About Me
I'm Chenyu Bi, a second-year M.S. student in Biostatistics at the University of Pennsylvania. Previously, I received my B.S. in Applied Statistics from the [School of Statistics](http://stat.ruc.edu.cn/EN/index.htm), [Renmin University of China (RUC)](https://en.ruc.edu.cn).

## Research Interests
My research focuses on advancing statistical methods for AI in biomedicine. I am interested in statistical machine learning, risk prediction modeling, trustworthy AI, and Bayesian optimization, with applications in electronic health records, protein engineering, and clinical decision support. My goal is to develop reliable, interpretable, and data-efficient methodologies that enable robust biomedical discovery and improve healthcare decision making.
